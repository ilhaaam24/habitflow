## English (US)

### Short Description (Max 80 characters)
Track habits daily with local data privacy and AI-powered insights.

### Full Description (Max 4000 characters)
⚡ WELCOME TO HABITFLOW AI: THE NO-NONSENSE HABIT TRACKER ⚡

Tired of soft, boring habit trackers that let you slide? Meet HabitFlow AI—the high-contrast, Neobrutalist-designed productivity hub that combines brutal honesty, smart local analytics, and gamified achievements to help you build habits that stick.

No subscriptions. No sign-ups. No data tracking. Just pure progress.

🤖 THE AI COACH: BRUTALLY HONEST MOTIVATION
HabitFlow AI is powered by Google Gemini (using your own API key). Our AI coach doesn't sugarcoat. It analyzes your real habits, streak patterns, and skipped days to deliver:
• Brutally honest daily motivation and insights in ALL CAPS.
• Weekly summary reports that call out your weak spots and praise your wins.
• Smart, localized recommendations to help you overcome stagnation.

🔑 BRING YOUR OWN KEY (BYOK)
We believe AI should be accessible and private. Input your own Gemini API key to unlock the coach. Your key is stored securely in your device's local settings using encryption, and it communicates directly with Google's API. No intermediate servers, no subscriptions, no markups.

📊 BOLD, DATA-FORWARD ANALYTICS
Understand your progress with our vibrant Neobrutalist charts:
• Weekly completion bar charts showing exactly how consistent you are.
• 4-week completion rate line charts to track trends.
• Category distribution donut charts to balance your lifestyle.
• A 52-week contribution grid (GitHub style) to visualize your long-term dedication.

🏆 INTERACTIVE BADGE SYSTEM
Level up your life! Unlock 7 custom-designed achievements based on your consistency and dedication:
• 🔥 First Flame - Complete your first habit.
• ⚡ 3-Day Warrior - Maintain a 3-day streak.
• 🏆 Week Champion - Maintain a 7-day streak.
• 💎 Month Master - Hit a 30-day streak.
• 🎯 Perfectionist - Complete all habits in a single day.
• 🌟 Multi-Tasker - Keep 5+ active habits running.
• 🚀 AI Explorer - Setup your AI connection.

🔒 LOCAL-FIRST & ZERO TELEMETRY
Your data belongs to you. HabitFlow AI is completely offline-first:
• Everything is stored locally on your device (SQLite & Hive databases).
• Zero tracking, zero telemetry, zero analytics scripts.
• Fully functional without an internet connection (except for AI queries).
• Easy offline backups and data exports.

🎨 NEOBRUTALIST STYLING
Break away from boring minimal layouts. HabitFlow AI features a gorgeous, retro-modern control panel interface with thick black borders, stark drop shadows, saturated accent colors, and bouncy micro-animations that make checking off a habit feel incredibly satisfying.

⚡ SELAMAT DATANG DI HABITFLOW AI: PELACAK KEBIASAAN TANPA BASA-BASI ⚡

Bosan dengan pelacak kebiasaan biasa yang membosankan? Sambut HabitFlow AI—aplikasi produktivitas bergaya Neobrutalisme kontras tinggi yang menggabungkan motivasi jujur dari AI, analisis lokal yang cerdas, dan pencapaian lencana interaktif untuk membantu Anda membangun kebiasaan yang langgeng.

Tanpa langganan. Tanpa pendaftaran. Tanpa pelacakan data. Hanya progres nyata.

🤖 ASISTEN AI: MOTIVASI JUJUR YANG BOLD
HabitFlow AI ditenagai oleh Google Gemini (menggunakan API key Anda sendiri). Pelatih AI kami tidak akan memanjakan Anda. AI akan menganalisis kebiasaan nyata, pola streak, dan hari-hari yang terlewat untuk memberikan:
• Motivasi harian yang jujur dan blak-blakan dengan huruf KAPITAL.
• Laporan mingguan yang menyoroti kelemahan dan merayakan pencapaian Anda.
• Rekomendasi lokal yang cerdas untuk mengatasi stagnasi.

🔑 SISTEM BYOK (BAWA API KEY ANDA SENDIRI)
Kami percaya AI harus dapat diakses secara gratis dan privat. Masukkan API key Gemini Anda sendiri untuk mengaktifkan pelatih AI. API key disimpan dengan aman di penyimpanan lokal perangkat Anda dengan enkripsi, dan terhubung langsung ke API Google. Tanpa server perantara, tanpa biaya langganan tambahan.

📊 ANALISIS DATA BOLD & INFORMATIF
Pahami progres Anda dengan grafik Neobrutalisme yang hidup:
• Grafik batang penyelesaian mingguan untuk memantau konsistensi.

🏆 SISTEM LENCANA PENCAPAIAN INTERAKTIF

• 🔥 First Flame - Menyelesaikan kebiasaan pertama.
• ⚡ 3-Day Warrior - Menjaga streak selama 3 hari.
• 🏆 Week Champion - Menjaga streak selama 7 hari.
• 💎 Month Master - Menjaga streak selama 30 hari.
• 🎯 Perfectionist - Menyelesaikan semua kebiasaan dalam satu hari.
• 🌟 Multi-Tasker - Memiliki 5+ kebiasaan aktif.

Data Anda sepenuhnya milik Anda. HabitFlow AI berjalan 100% offline-first:
