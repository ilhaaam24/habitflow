Privacy Policy

HabitFlow AI — Control Your Experience
LAST UPDATED: MAY 28, 2026

1. Local-First Architecture

100% Offline Database

HabitFlow AI is designed as a local-first application. We do not maintain user databases on external servers, and we do not track or collect your personal information.

All data generated by your use of the application—including habit setups, daily completion logs, streaks, category information, statistics, and unlocked achievements—is stored entirely on your device. We use secure SQLite and Hive databases located in your device's protected local app directory.

2. Gemini AI Key Security (BYOK)

Bring Your Own Key

Our AI Coaching and Daily Motivation systems operate under a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) model. To access AI features, you provide your own Google Gemini API key. Here is how your key is handled:

  • Local Storage: Your API key is saved directly inside your device's secure SharedPreferences. It never leaves your device except to query Google's APIs.
  • No Middleman Server: HabitFlow AI does not route your AI requests through any intermediary server or proxy. All network connections are sent directly from your device to Google's official Gemini API endpoint (https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com).
  • Prompt Context: The AI prompts are compiled locally on your device, using only your local habit names, category designations, and completion rates to generate personalized coaching feedback. No personal identifiers (such as names or emails) are sent.

3. Third-Party Services & Telemetry

We respect your privacy and attention. Therefore, HabitFlow AI has:

  • Zero Analytics: No tracking SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or Amplitude) are integrated.
  • Zero Crash Reporting Telemetry: No background error upload tools are present.
  • Zero Advertisements: We do not show ads, and no ad SDKs are loaded.

The only network operations performed by the application are direct queries to Google's API when requesting AI Motivations or Weekly Summaries, and checking external links (such as fetching your Gemini API key from Google AI Studio) when initiated by you.

4. Data Ownership & Control

Because we do not store your data on our servers, we have no access to it. This means:

  • No Account Required: You do not need to register an email or password to use HabitFlow AI.
  • Deletion: You can completely erase all your records at any time by clearing the application's data in your Android settings, or by uninstalling the application.
  • Exports: You can use our built-in export features inside the Settings panel to back up or download your database manually.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this local privacy policy, feel free to contact us via the project's repository issues board.
